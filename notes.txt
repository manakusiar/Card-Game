What can a player do?:
	1. Starting phase: Play cards.
	2. Main phase: Make choices of what cards will do when triggered.
	3. End phase:
		a. Discard down to 7.

What can cards do?:
	1. Attack --- Deal damage to the nearest enemy in front. (Check for keywords)
	2. Move --- Move left, right, up or down.
	3. Use their abilities --- Do whatever is written on the card.

What areas of play are there?:
	1. The deck / decks --- Holds the cards that the players draw and use.
	2. The battlefield:
		a. 4x2 field for creatures.
		b. 2-3 for items.
	3. The graveyard --- holds all killed creatures (sacrificed and enemies)
	4. The players hands --- infinite area, holds playable cards that you must discard down to 7 on the endstep.

What happens automatically?:
	1. Main phase:
		a. The triggering and prompting of card actions of every card depending on their speed.
		b. Resolving any matching speeds through coin flip.
	2. End phase:
		a. Clean up --- until end of turn effects end, any "on end step / phase" effects trigger.

Where to start?:
	1. Create a array / grid system per player for each area of play, then make them automatically sync with the server.
	2. Add a display to the grid global system that creates interractible cards.
